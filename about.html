Who we are

Built for South Africa, by South Africans

Taskr started with a simple observation: talented people with real skills, cleaners, tutors, plumbers, gardeners, were struggling to find consistent work, while households couldn't find reliable help they could trust. We built the bridge.

Join Taskr Get in touch
🤝
The problem we solve

South Africa has talent. It just needs a platform.

With an unemployment rate that disproportionately affects skilled workers in informal sectors, Taskr is designed to turn household service skills into consistent, dignified income, while giving families the reliable help they need.

🔍

Transparency

Every worker shows proof of their actual past work. No fake portfolios, no hidden profiles, just real results you can evaluate before booking.

🛡

Safety first

All workers on Taskr go through ID verification. Our trust and safety systems protect both customers and service providers at every step.

🌍

Community impact

We focus on connecting work with communities that need it most, building economic pathways in townships and informal areas across South Africa.

💬

Fair pricing

Workers set their own rates. We take a small platform fee only when a booking is completed, no hidden charges, no subscription traps.

📱

Simple to use

Whether you're 22 or 62, Taskr is built to be intuitive, fast and accessible, even on a basic smartphone connection.

⭐

Reputation matters

Our review system ensures quality stays high. Workers build real reputations, and customers benefit from the honest feedback of others.

Ready to make a difference?

Whether you need help or want to offer it — Taskr is your platform.
